Kiruna to Stockholm
For Travellers

Day 1 - Arrive at Kiruna

14 Feb 2020

Kiruna is a town in the far north of Swedish Lapland, known for its extraordinary geographical location. Here, 200 km north of the Arctic Circle, you will experience contrasts from Northern Lights to the midnight sun, the highest mountain in Sweden to the world's largest underground iron-ore mine, ancient Sámi culture to an ongoing city transformation. Check in to hotel and in the evening, enjoy chasing Northern Lights with husky tour.

Day 2 - Visiting Ice Hotel

15 Feb 2020

Spend a good morning and then start exploring with the world’s first and largest hotel made of ice and snow, the famous Ice Hotel. Each year it’s reincarnated in a new guise, carefully designed and hand crafted by some 40 artists from around the world. Indulge in indigenous Sami culture and food. Visit wooden Kiruna Church in the shape of a Sami goahti and Kiruna City Hall. It is fantastic outing in the Torne River valley.

Day 3 - Visiting Northern Lights

16 Feb 2020

You can choose to start this day with the heavenly aurora spa. The highlight of this day is visit to the STF Aurora Sky Station at Abisko where there is a large outdoor terrace and a lookout tower, where you can stand gazing at the Northern Lights and the stars. Inside is a Northern Lights exhibition that explains the Aurora Borealis. If you want to learn more about the Northern Lights, you can join one of the Northern Light presentations held by the guide several times during the evening.

Day 4 - Kiruna to Stockholm

17 Feb 2020

Take a morning flight to Stockholm. After checking in enjoy strolling through the famous alleys of the city. Stockholm subway stations are known for being super artistic. Spend sometime at the Djurgårdsslätten island! There you will find some of the most famous attractions in Stockholm, places to visit and have fun.

Day 5 - Stockholm

18 Feb 2020

After breakfast your city tour begins. Visit the City Hall, Sergel Square, Old Town. Special attention deserves Vasa Museum – it is the only preserved seventeenth-century ship in the world and a unique art treasure. Spend some time on the slopes or ice-covered lakes of Sweden. If you are lucky, experience the changing of guards ceremony at the royal palace.

Day 6 - Departure from Stockholm

19 Feb 2020

Departure from Stockholm and ending your pleasant Sweden Trip! We will be happy to see you again in The Wanderloo Trails!
